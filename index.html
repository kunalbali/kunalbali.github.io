About

I am a PhD student in Environmental Chemistry at the University of Alaska Fairbanks, Department of Chemistry and Biochemistry and Geophysical Institute, under the guidance of Dr. Jingqiu Mao . My current research focuses on understanding the optical properties and composition of atmospheric aerosols, particularly black carbon (BC) and brown carbon (BrC), with an emphasis on summer wildfire pollution and winter pollution in high-latitude environments.

Nationality: Indian | Gender: Male | Location: Fairbanks, Alaska, USA
